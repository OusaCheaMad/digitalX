Don't Wait - Apply Now through May 31st!

The Digital X programme is reviewing applications on a rolling, first come, first serve basis

Digital X finds proven, innovative digital solutions and connects them to UNDP in 170 countries with a chance to partner on projects of up to $100,000.

‍

Any team with a proven, ready-to-scale digital solution can apply to a DX Global Challenge and be listed in the DX Solutions Catalogue. This includes UNDP or UN teams with digital solutions, registered social enterprises, universities, private sector companies, NGOs, and nonprofits.
‍

Applications for the DX Global Challenge and DX Solutions Catalogue will close May 31, 2022.

Only UNDP Country Offices can apply for funding in partnership with teams or digital solutions that are first listed in the DX Solutions Catalogue starting June 01, 2022.
